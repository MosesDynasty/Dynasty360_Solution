How to Get a Visa to Canada in 2025

Written by Dynasty360 Travel Experts | Updated July 2025

Getting a visa to Canada can be simple if you understand the steps. In this guide, we’ll walk you through everything you need:

1. Determine the Type of Visa

Tourist, study, work — each has its own requirements. Start by identifying your purpose.

2. Prepare Key Documents

  • Valid international passport
  • Bank statement (6 months)
  • Letter of invitation (if visiting)
  • Proof of ties to Nigeria

3. Apply Online

Visit Canada’s official portal and fill out the online form.
